Validica: Smart Compliance

Validica is a consultancy firm specializing in the pharmaceutical industry, Quality Management Systems, and Regulatory Compliance. We help companies translate regulatory requirements into practical, effective solutions.

At Validica, we believe that GMP is not a one-size-fits-all framework. We apply our regulatory knowledge, technical understanding, and inspection experience to design tailored approaches for different products, processes, and facilities that meet regulatory expectations.

We work with a phase-appropriate GMP mindset, adjusting the control practices and structure to the development stage of your drug product — ensuring compliance that is scientifically justified and risk-based.

That is what we call Smart Compliance: a way of working where regulatory rigor meets innovative thinking.

About

Laura Moreno

Pharma Quality & Regulatory Consultant | GMP Compliance | EU QP

I am a GMP compliance expert with a dedicated career to the pharmaceutical industry, specializing in GMP Quality and Regulatory requirements, validation, and certified EU Qualified Person (QP) under Directive 2001/83/EC.

I have led Quality Systems across the full product lifecycle — from API and Drug Product manufacturing to aseptic operations, inspection readiness, and commercial release. I support GMP certifications from scratch, FDA/EMA inspection readiness programs within CDMO environments, remediation and Warning Letter support.

My technical focus includes process validation (PPQ), cleaning and analytical validation, batch certification, and risk-based quality management. I work with a practical, science-driven approach to ensure decisions are justified, traceable, and inspection-ready.

I founded Validica to deliver smart, tailored GMP compliance — combining technical rigor with pragmatic, scalable solutions that support innovation.

My background includes a PhD in Medicinal Chemistry and a career path from Quality Control Specialist to QA Director and Qualified Person. I believe strong quality comes from understanding the rules and applying them with judgment.
